Project Synopsis
The Emirates Unlock Europe interactive marketing campaign ran nationwide in a bid to build a customer database of potential customers interested in first-class global travel. Users took an online journey through 22 European destinations answering questions at each European pit-stop. Users who successfully answered each of the questions were eligible to win a trip for 2 - first class around the world to 22 destinations.
Key Accomplishments
Cloud Gurus planned, created, built, deployed, hosted and maintained the Unlock Emirates campaign end-to-end. The objective was to invite users into an authentic online European experience and give them a taste of European travel. Using clever design architecture and merged imagery, we were able to engage a staggering number of potential customers and convert them into new database entries for Emirates.
